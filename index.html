Every year, hundreds of laws pass through Westminster. Most people never hear about them until it's too late. Bills. tracks every piece of legislation in plain English — filtered to the things that actually matter to you.

Why we built this
We were tired of finding out about laws that affected our lives after they'd already passed. A friend couldn't believe a bill changing their rights had been debated for months without them knowing. So we built Bills. — the tracker we wish had always existed.
